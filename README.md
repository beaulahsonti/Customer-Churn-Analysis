# Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview

Customer Churn Analysis is a data analytics project designed to identify customer retention patterns and understand the factors that contribute to customer attrition. The project uses data analysis and visualization techniques to uncover business insights that can help organizations improve customer retention strategies.

---

## Problem Statement

Customer churn directly impacts business growth and profitability. Understanding why customers leave enables organizations to take proactive measures to increase retention and improve customer satisfaction.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior.
* Identify factors influencing customer attrition.
* Visualize churn trends across customer segments.
* Generate actionable business insights.
* Support data-driven decision making.

---

## Key Insights

* Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn rate.
* Long-term contract customers were more likely to remain with the company.
* Customer tenure had a significant impact on retention.
* Contract type emerged as one of the strongest predictors of churn.
